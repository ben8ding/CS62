# SDLC (Software Developmment Lifecycle) notes
* "You just covered my entire marketing course in 40 mins" - some doofus
* Requirements -> Design -> Implementation -> Testing
* Requirements process: Interview -> Persona and Scenarios -> Define Requirements
* Questions to ask: Who are you helping?"
* Personas: Create fictional character/customer constructed from interviews
* Personas have: Goals, needs, desires. Help define solutions and frustrations
* 1 Primary Persona with some secondary personas. If conflict of interest, primary persona wins.
* Minimum Viable Product (Easy to build, able to be demo'ed)
